RETURN & EXCHANGE

Have you received my return?

Once your returning package has been received at our warehouse, it is processed and inspected before we proceed in approving or disapproving the respective return. We appreciate your understanding that, although we work as quickly as possible, it is possible that this process may take 1-3 business days to complete.

−

When will I receive my refund?

Once your returning package has been received at our warehouse, it is processed and inspected before we proceed in approving or disapproving the respective return. Your return will be processed within a few business days after it arrives in our warehouse.

+

Will the shipping costs for my return parcel be covered by IDOL?

Your return shipping cost will be borne by yourself if you return your order using the Self Arrange option. Shopee will not cover any shipping cost for parcels returned overseas. For overseas orders, you will be provided with a local address to shop your return parcel to. Do not ship the return parcel directly to an overseas address.

+

How many days are buyers allowed to raise return request for perishable items?

IDOL allows refund requests for perishable items within 3 days of delivery. Any requests raised after this period will not be entertained as Shopee would be unable to assess the condition of the item.

+

What is your return policy?

You have the right to return products purchased directly from danielwellington.com within 30 days* of receiving the item(s) for a full refund.

The product(s) must be unused which mean in original packaging with labels and protective stickers intact - and still in the same, new condition as when received.

Products purchased directly from danielwellington.com can be returned to our warehouse using a pre-paid shipping label, which we will provide. Kindly note that items cannot be returned to a local retail store.
